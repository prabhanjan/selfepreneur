• ABC of selfepreneurship.

    Selfpreneuer: Being a selfpreneuer is being self sufficient or self-independent. In life there comes certain instances where we have to learn alone and go through the hardships alone. During this time we learn from our experience and environment. It is very important that we stay updated with what is happening in the world and brush up on our strengths. As we grow up, we may go through some tough obstacles that will make us a strong, independent person. But sometimes life happens and we lose our sense of independence.
